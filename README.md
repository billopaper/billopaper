## What is a BilloPaper?

A **BilloPaper** is like a research paper that skipped the formalities.

A regular paper demands related work, novelty, and rigor before you're allowed to be curious. A BilloPaper drops all of that. The idea doesn't have to be original. There's no literature review. There's no reviewer to please.

There's just a question that sparked my curiosity — and an honest, detailed answer.

> **Billo** *(German slang)*: cheap, scrappy, no-frills — but surprisingly effective. 🛠️

---

## The BilloPaper Format

Every BilloPaper follows roughly the same structure:

| | Section | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| ❓ | **Question** | The original curiosity. Plainly stated. |
| ⚡ | **Short Answer** | The quick takeaway, for impatient readers. |
| 🔧 | **Setup** | Architecture, datasets, design decisions. |
| 🧪 | **Implementation** | The actual code, in a public repository. |
| 📊 | **Résumé** | Results, lessons learned, and final thoughts. |

That's it. No citations, no related work, no future work, no pretending the idea is revolutionary.

---

## Why BilloPapers Exist

**Curiosity > Novelty**
The idea doesn't need to be original. It just needs to be interesting enough to try.

**Show > Tell**
Every paper is backed by real code, real experiments, and public repos.

**Honest by Default**
If something breaks, fails, or underperforms, that stays in.

**Low Ceremony, High Signal**
Read in minutes. Fork freely.

**Prototype-Focused**
BilloPapers don't try to be perfect. It's about fast ideation, testing ideas and bringing them to life as quickly as possible. Shortcuts are welcome.
